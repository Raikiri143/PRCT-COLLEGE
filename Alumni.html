Dean

Pablo Joshua is Dean, Reliance Professor of Management and Private Enterprise and Professor of Management at the PRCT College, and Professor of Political Science at the University of Bangbang. He became Dean of the PRCT COllege in 2018, having been a member of the PRCt College faculty in the Management Department from 1995 to 1997.
